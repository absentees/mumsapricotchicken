Mum's Apricot Chicken

  • 405ml Berri Apricot Nectar
  • 1½ kg whole chicken, cut into 8 pieces
  • 1 pkt French onion soup, save a little for the onion
  • 1 sliced onion
  1. Roll chicken pieces in flour until well coated. Place into caserole dish.
  2. Heat together nectar and soup until thickened.
  3. Pour over chicken pieces.
  4. Top with sliced onion and remaing french onion soup.
  5. Cover and bake in a moderate oven (180C) for 1-1½ hours.
